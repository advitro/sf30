# SF30 V2.0 — Installation Guide

## Overview

SF30 V2.0 is a Chrome Extension that automates shift polling and claiming on Amazon AtoZ. This guide covers installation, activation, and basic usage.

---

## Step 1: Prepare the Extension Files

1. Download `SF30-V2.0.zip` (73 KB)
2. Extract the zip file to a permanent location on your computer:
   - **Windows**: `C:\Users\<YourName>\SF30\`
   - **macOS**: `~/Applications/SF30/`
   - **Linux**: `~/SF30/`

> ⚠️ **Important**: Do NOT delete this folder after installation. Chrome loads the extension directly from this location.

---

## Step 2: Enable Developer Mode in Chrome

1. Open Google Chrome
2. Type `chrome://extensions/` in the address bar and press Enter
3. Toggle **"Developer mode"** ON (top-right corner)

---

## Step 3: Load the Extension

1. Click **"Load unpacked"** button (top-left)
2. Navigate to the folder where you extracted SF30-V2.0
3. Select the folder and click **"Select Folder"**
4. The SF30 extension will appear in your extensions list

You should see:
- Extension name: "SF30 V2.0"
- Version: 2.0.0
- ID: (auto-generated by Chrome)

---

## Step 4: Pin the Extension (Optional)

1. Click the puzzle piece icon 🧩 in Chrome toolbar
2. Find "SF30 V2.0" in the list
3. Click the pin icon 📌 to keep it visible

---

## Step 5: Activate Your License

1. Click the **SF30 icon** in your Chrome toolbar
2. Read the **Privacy Notice** and click **"Accept & Continue"**
3. Check the **"I am 16 years or older"** checkbox
4. Your **Device Fingerprint** will be displayed
5. **Copy the fingerprint** and send it to the license provider
6. You will receive a **license key** bound to your device
7. Paste the license key in the input field
8. Click **"Verify"**
9. Once verified, toggle **"Shift Grabber"** ON

---

## Step 6: Configure Settings

### Shift Dates
- Click the **"Shifts"** tab
- Use the date picker to add dates you want to claim
- Click **"Add"** after each date
- Use **"Clear All"** to remove all dates

### Blacklist
- Click the **"Blacklist"** tab
- Add dates you want to **skip** (e.g., days you know you can't work)

### Telegram Notifications (Optional)
- Click the **"Settings"** tab
- Enter your Telegram Bot Token and Chat ID
- Click **"Save"**
- You will receive a notification every time a shift is claimed

---

## Step 7: Open Amazon AtoZ

1. Navigate to `https://atoz.amazon.work`
2. The extension will automatically inject its content scripts
3. A small **HUD** ( Heads-Up Display ) will appear in the top-right corner
4. The HUD shows: status, polls count, claimed count, and timer

## Troubleshooting

### Extension not showing on AtoZ
- Make sure the extension is **enabled** (toggle is ON)
- Refresh the AtoZ page
- Check that you're on `https://atoz.amazon.work` or `https://atoz-apps.amazon.work`

### License verification fails
- Ensure you copied the **entire fingerprint** correctly
- The license key is **device-bound** — it won't work on another computer
- Contact support with your fingerprint if issues persist

### No shifts being claimed
- Verify you have **dates selected** in the Shifts tab
- Check that those dates are **not blacklisted**
- Ensure your **license is valid** (not expired)
- Make sure you're on the AtoZ **shift opportunities** page

### Telegram notifications not working
- Verify your **Bot Token** is correct (format: `123456789:ABCdef...`)
- Verify your **Chat ID** is correct
- Send a test message to your bot first to activate it

## Updating the Extension

When a new version is released:

1. Delete the old extension folder
2. Extract the new zip file to the same location
3. Go to `chrome://extensions/`
4. Find SF30 and click the **refresh** icon 🔄

---

## Uninstalling

1. Go to `chrome://extensions/`
2. Find "SF30 V2.0"
3. Click **"Remove"**
4. (Optional) Delete the extension folder from your computer
