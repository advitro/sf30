# Shift Grabber V9 — Troubleshooting

## Common Issues

### "OFF" Badge (Red)
**What it means:** The extension is disabled.

**Fix:**
1. Click the SG icon
2. Flip the big toggle to **ON**
3. Make sure your license key is entered and valid

---

### "License Invalid" or "License Expired"
**What it means:** Your key is wrong, expired, or the verification server can't be reached.

**Fix:**
1. Double-check you copied the full key (no extra spaces before/after)
2. Click the SG icon → go to **Advanced settings**
3. Click **Verify** next to the license key field
4. Make sure you have an internet connection
5. If it still fails, contact support with your key

---

### Extension Not Grabbing Shifts
**What it means:** The extension is active but not claiming.

**Fix:**
1. Make sure you're on the **AtoZ schedule page** (URL starts with `https://atoz.amazon.work`)
2. Check the HUD in the bottom-right — it should say **LIVE** (green)
3. If it says **PAUSED**, press **P** to resume
4. Try pressing **R** to reload the page
5. Make sure the date you're looking at isn't in your blacklist

---

### HUD Not Showing
**What it means:** The status overlay is hidden or failed to inject.

**Fix:**
1. Press **Shift + H** while on an AtoZ page
2. Reload the page (F5 or **R**)
3. Check that the extension is enabled (badge should not say OFF)

---

### "Extension Disabled by Chrome"
**What it means:** Chrome turned off the extension, usually after an update or restart.

**Fix:**
1. Go to `chrome://extensions`
2. Find **Shift Grabber V9**
3. Click the toggle to turn it back **ON**
4. Re-enter your license key if needed

---

### Slow or Missed Shifts
**What it means:** Shifts appear but aren't claimed fast enough.

**Fix:**
1. Press **Shift + O** to enable **Override (FAST)** mode
2. Press **Shift + T** for turbo mode
3. Open multiple date tabs so the extension polls more windows
4. Close other Chrome tabs/extensions to free up resources

---

### "Payment Failed" / "Past Due"
**What it means:** Your subscription payment didn't go through.

**Fix:**
1. Click the SG icon
2. Click **Manage Billing**
3. Update your payment method in the billing portal
4. Return to the extension and click **Verify** to re-activate

---

### Extension Icon Missing from Toolbar
**Fix:**
1. Click the **puzzle piece** icon (extensions menu) in Chrome toolbar
2. Find **Shift Grabber V9**
3. Click the **pin icon** (📌) next to it

---

## Still Stuck?
Contact support using the **"Need help?"** link inside the extension popup, or reach out via the channel where you purchased your license.
